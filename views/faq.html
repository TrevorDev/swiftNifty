Nifty Kick takes 9.9% of all purchases. No additional fees.

Why did you create Nifty Kick?

As artists we were fed up with the current methods of selling our creations. We created Nifty Kick to make it more intuitive to share our work with the world without breaking the bank.

Who does this money go to?

2.9% + 30 cents goes to payment processing powered by Stripe.
The rest goes to NiftyKick to keep the site running/pay for hosting.

How do your prices compare to others?

Other sites we have looked at take anywhere from %15 to %30 and for some that doesn't even include payment processing fees. Let us know if you find any cheaper alternatives.

How do I get paid?

Currently we will mail you a check within a month of payment request if your owed value is over $50.

How do I get a job working for Nifty Kick?

We are always looking for great talent! Send an email with your resume to niftykick@gmail.com

Will Bitcoin ever be accepted on NiftyKick?

We have plans allow Bitcoin as a payment method in the future.

How do I contact the great people of Nifty Kick?

You can reach us at niftykick@gmail.com
